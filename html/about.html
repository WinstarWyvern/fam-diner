About Us

Founded in 2001, FamDiner starter as a humble small kitchen in Kemanggisan, Jakarta serving rice dishes to local population. As time progresses, more customers come to visit our restaurant, and so we decided to expand our menu and location to other area and provinces. Today, we can proudly say that we opened 75+ locations spread in Sumatra and Java Island. We have a commitment to stay true to our humble origin which is to serve customers with high-quality food so that they can leave with an unforgettable experience.
